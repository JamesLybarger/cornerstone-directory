## Plain-English Summary

Before you read the full policy, here's the short version:

- **We collect what you give us** — your name, email, and business info when you register or create a listing.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

When you interact with Cornerstone Directory, you may give us:

- **Account registration data** — your name, email address, and a password (which we store only as a secure hash — we never see your actual password).
- **Business listing data** — your business name, description, category, location, contact information, website URL, and any photos you upload. This information is displayed publicly in the directory.
- **Referral information** — if you participate in our referral program, we track referral codes, referral credits, and records of members referred by you.
- **Communications** — if you email us at contact@cornerstonedirectory.com, we retain that correspondence to help resolve your inquiry.
- **Store purchases** — if you purchase a digital product (e-books, downloads, or other resources) from our store, we record what you purchased in order to fulfill and support that transaction.

### Payment Information

All payments are processed securely by **Stripe**, a trusted third-party payment processor. Cornerstone Directory does not collect, see, or store your credit card number, billing address, or other sensitive financial details. Stripe handles all of that directly. You can review Stripe's privacy practices at [stripe.com/privacy](https://stripe.com/privacy).

We do retain a record of your membership type, payment status, renewal dates, and referral credits in our own systems so we can manage your account.

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit Cornerstone Directory, some information is collected automatically:

- **Usage data** — pages you visit, time spent on the site, links you click, and how you navigate the platform.
- **Device and browser data** — your browser type, operating system, IP address, and referring URL.

This data is collected primarily through **Google Analytics** and helps us understand how people use the platform so we can improve it. Google Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated — we cannot use it to identify you personally.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Operate the platform** — create and manage your account, process your membership, and give you access to the features you've paid for.
- **Display your business listing** — your listing information is published publicly in the directory so that visitors can find and contact your business. This is the core purpose of the platform.
- **Process payments** — we pass transaction information to Stripe to complete purchases and manage subscriptions.
- **Send transactional emails** — we use Resend to deliver emails such as welcome messages, purchase confirmations, referral notifications, and renewal reminders. These are not marketing emails; they are necessary to operate your account.
- **Manage referrals** — we track referral codes and credits so we can award the correct $4.99 credit when someone you referred becomes an annual member.
- **Improve the platform** — analytics data helps us understand what's working and what isn't, so we can build a better product.
- **Respond to your support requests** — when you contact us, we use your information to help you.

We do not use your information for any purpose other than running this platform and serving you well.

## 5. Business Directory Listings

This section is worth calling out clearly because it's unique to what we do.

When you create a business listing on Cornerstone Directory, **that listing is intentionally public.** Anyone who visits the site — member or not — can view your:

- Business name
- Business description and category
- Location (city/region as you enter it)
- Contact information (phone, email, website, as you choose to provide)
- Photos you upload

**This is by design.** The whole point of the directory is for people to find your business. Please only include information in your listing that you are comfortable sharing publicly.

If you close your account or ask us to delete your listing, we will remove it from the public directory promptly. However, we cannot control whether third parties (search engines, web archives) may have cached your listing before removal.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as long as needed to provide you services.

- **Active accounts** — we keep your account data, listing data, and purchase history for the duration of your membership.
- **Deleted accounts** — when you delete your account, we remove your personal information from our active systems within 30 days. Some anonymized or aggregated data (e.g., analytics) may remain, but it cannot be linked back to you.
- **Communications** — we may retain support emails for up to 2 years for record-keeping and quality purposes.
- **Payment records** — Stripe retains transaction records in accordance with their own retention policy and applicable financial regulations.

## 7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. These measures include:

- Passwords are stored as hashed values — we cannot read your actual password.
- Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe, which maintains PCI-DSS compliance.
- Our platform is hosted on Railway, which provides secure infrastructure.
- We use HTTPS to encrypt data in transit between your browser and our servers.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we work hard to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at contact@cornerstonedirectory.com.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

You have meaningful control over your information:

- **Access** — you can request a summary of the personal information we hold about you.
- **Correction** — if any of your information is inaccurate, you can update it in your account settings or ask us to correct it.
- **Deletion** — you can delete your account at any time. We will remove your personal data from our active systems within 30 days of your request.
- **Data portability** — you can request a copy of your data in a readable format.
- **Opt out of marketing emails** — if we ever send promotional emails, you can opt out at any time using the unsubscribe link. Note that transactional emails (such as renewal reminders and purchase confirmations) are necessary to operate your account and cannot be turned off while your account is active.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@cornerstonedirectory.com. We will respond within a reasonable time, typically within 30 days.

### California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- **Right to Know** — you may request details about the categories of personal information we collect, use, disclose, or sell (we do not sell personal information).
- **Right to Delete** — you may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
- **Right to Non-Discrimination** — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights. Exercising your rights will not affect your access to the platform.
- **Right to Opt Out of Sale** — we do not sell personal information, so there is nothing to opt out of. However, you may submit a request to confirm this at any time.

To make a CCPA request, contact us at contact@cornerstonedirectory.com with the subject line "CCPA Privacy Request." We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

Cornerstone Directory uses cookies and similar technologies to operate the platform and understand how it's used.

- **Essential cookies** — required for core functionality such as keeping you logged in and remembering your session.
- **Analytics cookies** — Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymized data about how visitors use the site (pages visited, session duration, etc.). This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

You can control or block cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to use the platform. To opt out of Google Analytics tracking specifically, you can use the [Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to ad networks.
