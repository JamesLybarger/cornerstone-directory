### Persona 1 — "Faith-Forward Freelancer" — **Grace M.**

**Demographics:** Woman, 34, Knoxville, TN. Self-employed Christian life/business coach and virtual assistant. Two kids. Active in her local Baptist church.

**Pain Points:**
- Feels isolated — no peers who understand running a business AND walking with God
- Undercharges and struggles with pricing because it "doesn't feel right" to talk about money at church

- Has never had a business mentor she could fully be herself with

**Goals:**
- Grow revenue to $75K without compromising her values
- Find a community of women who "get it" — faith and business together

- Learn from people further ahead on the path

**Where She Spends Time Online:** Facebook groups, Instagram Reels, Pinterest, email newsletters from faith-based influencers

**What Will Convert Her:** Testimonials from women like her; a free resource that immediately solves a real problem; seeing the community is active and encouraging, not just promotional

**Messaging Hook:** *"Stop trying to build your business alone. God didn't design you for isolation — and neither did we."*

## 3. Launch Strategy — First 500 Founding Member Campaign

### Campaign Name: **"Called to Build"**

### Campaign Tagline
*"Be among the first 500. Lock in lifetime access. Change your business forever."*

> *"Commit your work to the Lord, and your plans will be established."* — Proverbs 16:3

### Pre-Launch (Days 1–7): Build Anticipation

**Day 1 — Announce the Vision**
- Post a heartfelt founder story on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok explaining *why* you're building KBN — connect it to your journey in consulting, laser engraving, real estate, and your book "Soar Like an Eagle"
- Add a simple landing page (Netlify) with a "Get Notified at Launch" email capture
- Email your existing contacts and book readers personally — "I'm building something for people like you"

**Day 2 — Launch the Waiting List**
- Share a "founding member waitlist" opt-in across all channels
- Canva graphic: counter showing "500 spots — XX already claimed" (start at a realistic count)
- Direct DM anyone who has expressed interest in faith, business, or coaching topics previously

**Day 3 — Introduce Persona-Targeted Content**
- Post three pieces of content (one per day): one targeting freelancers/coaches, one for established business owners, one for side hustlers
- Use the hook: *"If you've ever felt like you had to leave your faith at the door of your business… this is for you."*

**Day 4 — Share Social Proof Setup**
- Post your personal authority story: your background in consulting, grant writing, real estate, laser engraving
- Mention the book: quote from "Soar Like an Eagle" — connect the eagle metaphor to business vision
- Ask your audience: "What's the #1 thing you wish you had when you started your business?" (comment bait; builds algorithm signal)

**Day 5 — Church & Network Outreach Begins**
- Begin personal outreach to 10–15 pastors, Christian business group leaders, and friends-of-friends in business (see Section 7)
- Ask them to be a Founding Member AND to share with their congregation/network

**Day 6 — Teaser: Show the Platform**
- Short video walkthrough (even Loom or phone screen recording) showing the member portal, directory, and resource library
- Create urgency: "Doors open in X days. Founding member price disappears when 500 spots are filled."

**Day 7 — Last Day of Waitlist**
- Final waitlist push: "Tomorrow, the doors open. Founding members get lifetime access for $29.99 — that's it, forever."
- Go Live on Facebook or Instagram to answer questions

---

### Launch Week (Days 8–14): Open Doors

**Day 8 — Official Launch**
- Send launch email to full waitlist (see Section 6 for email sequence)
- All social platforms: "We're LIVE. 500 founding member spots are open NOW."
- Pin a countdown post or Instagram Story countdown sticker
- Go live again; walk through what members get step by step

**Days 9–11 — Social Proof Sprint**
- Each day, feature one founding member who has joined (with permission): who they are, what they do, why they joined
- Post testimonials or direct quotes from early members
- Behind-the-scenes: "This is what the community looks like on Day 3"

**Day 12 — Milestone Celebration**
- Announce progress: "We've welcomed [X] founding members. [500 - X] spots remain."
- Urgency post: When the founding spots fill, the price goes from $29.99 lifetime to $39.99/yr — forever
- Tag 3 people publicly you'd love to see in the community (with permission or in a general way)

**Day 13 — Objection Busting**
- Address common hesitations in a post or video: "Is this just another Facebook group? No. Here's why..." — walk through the platform features
- Address the price: "$29.99 once. One Starbucks run a week for a month. Then you're in — forever."

**Day 14 — End of Week Recap**
- Email + social: "One week in. X members strong. Here's what's happening inside."

---

### Momentum Phase (Days 15–30): Sustain and Grow

**Days 15–21 — Content Value Blitz**
- Publish the first 2 blog posts from the SEO calendar (Section 5) — demonstrate the content you'll bring inside the community
- Share one piece of daily value content that existing members could share outward
- Run a referral push: "Tag a Christian entrepreneur who needs this" — every tag extends your organic reach

**Days 22–25 — Paid Ad Activation**
- Launch the Facebook/Instagram ad campaign (see Section 8) to cold audiences
- Target: Christian business owners, Christian entrepreneurs, faith-based professionals ages 28–55
- Budget: $10–$20/day for first week; scale what converts

**Days 26–28 — Partnership Amplification**
- Church bulletin announcements go live (set up in Days 1–7)
- Guest podcast appearances (set up in Week 1–2) begin to air or get scheduled
- Cross-promote with any Christian business associations or groups who agreed to share

**Days 29–30 — Final Countdown to 500**
- Public counter showing remaining founding spots (update daily)
- "Last chance" email sequence to unconverted waitlist members
- Final social push: "The founding member window is closing. After [date/number], the price changes forever."

---

1. **Lifetime access** at $29.99 — never pay again
2. **"Founding Member" badge** in the community — permanent recognition
3. **Priority access** to coaching calls and advisory sessions
4. **Name listed** in the KBN Founding Member Wall (website page)

## 7. Partnership & Referral Strategy

> *"Two are better than one, because they have a good return for their labor."* — Ecclesiastes 4:9

### Tier 1 — Church Partnerships

**Target:** Non-denominational, Baptist, Evangelical, and Pentecostal churches with active members in your region (Southern Utah, Nevada, Arizona) + nationally via online outreach.

**Pitch:** *"KBN equips your members to be marketplace ministers. We'd love to offer your congregation a free business directory listing and a 30-day trial resource as part of a church partnership."*

**How to Approach:**
1. Identify 10–20 local churches per month via Google and ChurchFinder
2. Contact the Pastor or Business/Ministry Leader directly — email + phone
3. Offer to speak at a business ministry event or men's/women's group
4. Provide a Church Partner Kit: bulletin insert template (Canva), QR code to free listing, 5 social media graphics for them to post
5. In return, ask for: bulletin mention, pulpit announcement (if appropriate), email to congregation, and a social post

**Church Partner Benefit:** Their congregation members get a custom landing page with church affiliate tag — the church can track how many members they helped join.

**Monthly Target:** 2–4 church partnership agreements per month

---

### Tier 2 — Christian Business Associations & Organizations

**Target organizations to approach:**
- Christian Chamber of Commerce chapters (local and national)
- CBMC (Christian Business Men's Connection) — national with local chapters
- Fellowship of Companies for Christ International (FCCI)
- National Christian Foundation business networking events
- Kingdom Business Association (national)
- Local faith-based business networking groups (search Facebook Groups in your region)

**Pitch:** *"Let's partner to serve the same audience better. We bring the platform and resources; you bring the credibility and community. Together, we can equip more Christian entrepreneurs."*

**Partnership Offer:**
- Affiliate revenue share: 20% of each membership sold through their link
- Co-branded resources (e.g., "KBN × [Org Name] Business Starter Kit")
- Guest post/feature swap
- Joint webinar

## 10. Long-Term Community Growth

> *"Plans fail for lack of counsel, but with many advisers they succeed."* — Proverbs 15:22

### Peer Advisory Groups ("Kingdom Circles")

**Structure:**
- Small groups of 6–10 members meeting virtually every 2 weeks via video call
- Each group organized by business stage: Starters (0–2 yrs), Builders (2–7 yrs), Scalers (7+ yrs)
- Each circle has a designated "Circle Leader" — a Founding Member who volunteers or is compensated
- Monthly agenda: 1 devotional, 1 hot seat (member presents a challenge), 1 strategy share, 1 prayer

**Launch Timeline:**
- Month 3: Launch first 2–3 pilot circles with founding members
- Month 6: Scale to 8–10 active circles
- Month 12: Formalize Circle Leader training program

**Why it drives retention:** Community is the #1 reason people stay. The peer advisory group makes members feel accountable, seen, and connected. When they consider canceling, they're not canceling a subscription — they're leaving their people.

### Annual Event: **"Kingdom Summit"**

**Concept:**
A 1-day hybrid (in-person + virtual) gathering for Kingdom Business Network members — a faith-meets-business conference built around the KBN community.

**Year 1 Goal:** Host a virtual-only event. Budget: $0 production cost by using Zoom webinar. Goal: 200 attendees from member community.

**Agenda Framework:**
- Morning: Worship/devotional keynote ("Building the Kingdom through business")
- Late Morning: Founder keynote + "State of the Network" report
- Midday: Member spotlight presentations (3 members share their Kingdom business story)
- Afternoon: Workshop tracks (2 parallel tracks: one for early-stage, one for established)
  - Workshop A: Launch Your First Digital Product

- Late Afternoon: Peer advisory "speed circles" — 8 minutes per group, meet new members
- Close: Group prayer, celebration of milestones, sneak peek of Year 2

**Sponsorship Opportunity:** Bring in 2–3 aligned faith-based brands or service providers as event sponsors ($500–$1,500 each) to offset costs and generate additional revenue.

**Year 2:** Consider a regional in-person event (Southern Utah or Phoenix) with 100–200 attendees. Sell tickets ($97–$197) as a separate revenue stream.
