# Refund Policy — Cornerstone Directory

**Last Updated: June 16, 2026**

---

## The Short Version (Plain English)

Here's what you need to know before reading the full policy:

- **Founding Members** get a 14-day refund window from the date of purchase. After that, the lifetime membership is final — no renewals, no future charges, no refunds.
- **Annual Members** can cancel anytime, but we don't issue prorated refunds for unused time. Your access continues through the end of your current billing period.
- **Digital products** (e-books, downloads, resources) are non-refundable once you've accessed or downloaded them — standard practice for digital goods.
- **Referral credits** you earned are forfeited if you request a refund on the membership that generated them.
- **Mistakes happen** — duplicate charges, billing errors, or technical problems are always made right, no questions asked. Just reach out to us directly.

## Full Refund Policy

### 1. Overview

Cornerstone Directory LLC ("Cornerstone Directory," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to being fair, transparent, and straightforward about how we handle refunds. This policy applies to all paid memberships and digital product purchases made through cornerstonedirectory.com. We want every member to feel confident joining our community — and we want to be honest about what we can and cannot offer in return.

If you have any questions not addressed here, please reach out before making a purchase. We're happy to help.

---

### 2. Membership Tiers — Refund Terms

#### 2a. Founding Member (One-Time Lifetime Payment — $59.99)

The Founding Member tier is a **one-time, lifetime payment** that grants permanent access to Cornerstone Directory for as long as the platform operates. There are no renewals, no annual charges, and no future fees.

Because this is a lifetime purchase at a deeply discounted rate, refunds are available under the following terms:

- **Refund window:** 14 days from the date of original purchase.
- **How to request:** Contact us at [contact@cornerstonedirectory.com] within the 14-day window with your name, email address, and order confirmation number.
- **After 14 days:** The purchase is final and non-refundable. Once the refund window closes, you retain lifetime access, and no refund will be issued for any reason other than a covered exception (see Section 6).

We encourage you to review the membership benefits carefully before purchasing. If you have questions about what's included, please ask us first.

#### 2b. Annual Member (Recurring Subscription — $59.99/year)

The Annual Member tier is a **recurring annual subscription** that renews automatically each year on your billing anniversary date.

- **Cancellation:** You may cancel your Annual Membership at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting us at [contact@cornerstonedirectory.com].
- **Access after cancellation:** Upon cancellation, your membership remains active through the end of the current paid billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation.
- **Prorated refunds:** We do **not** offer prorated refunds for partial subscription periods. If you cancel mid-year, you retain access through the end of the period you paid for, but the unused portion of that period is not refunded.
- **Renewal charges:** If you did not intend to renew and were charged on your renewal date, please contact us within **7 days** of the charge. We will review your account activity and, at our discretion, issue a refund if the renewal had not yet been used or accessed.

We recommend canceling before your renewal date if you do not wish to continue your membership.

#### 2c. Free Member

Free memberships involve no payment and are therefore not subject to this refund policy.

### 3. Digital Product Purchases

Cornerstone Directory may offer digital products such as downloadable files, e-books, guides, or other digital resources (collectively, "Digital Products").

- **All Digital Product sales are final once the product has been accessed, downloaded, or delivered.**
- This policy exists because digital files cannot be "returned" in a meaningful way once received.
- If you purchased a Digital Product and experienced a technical issue that prevented you from accessing or downloading it, please contact us at [contact@cornerstonedirectory.com]. We will resolve the issue or issue a refund as appropriate.
- Accidental or duplicate purchases of Digital Products will be refunded if reported within 48 hours and the product has not been accessed.

---

### 4. Referral Credits

Cornerstone Directory may offer a referral program that allows members to earn credits toward future purchases or membership fees.

- Referral credits are earned when a referred member completes a qualifying purchase using your referral link.
- **If a member you referred requests and receives a refund, the referral credit associated with their purchase will be reversed or forfeited from your account.**
- **If you request a refund on your own membership**, any referral credits earned under that membership — whether used or unused — will be forfeited at the time the refund is processed.
- Credits have no cash value and cannot be transferred, sold, or redeemed for cash at any time.

---

### 5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

1. **Email us** at [contact@cornerstonedirectory.com] with the subject line: `Refund Request – [Your Full Name]`
2. **Include in your email:**
   - Your full name
   - The email address associated with your account
   - Your order confirmation number or transaction ID
   - The reason for your refund request
3. **Response time:** We aim to respond to all refund requests within **3 business days**. Approved refunds are typically processed within **5–10 business days**, depending on your payment provider.

Refunds, when approved, are issued to the original payment method used at the time of purchase. We are unable to redirect refunds to a different payment method.

### 6. Exceptions — When We'll Always Make It Right

Regardless of the terms above, we will issue a full refund or credit without question in the following situations:

- **Duplicate charge:** You were charged more than once for the same product or membership period.
- **Billing error:** A technical error resulted in an incorrect charge.
- **Unauthorized charge:** You did not authorize the transaction and have reason to believe your account was compromised. (We also recommend contacting your bank and changing your password immediately.)
- **Failed delivery:** A Digital Product could not be delivered or accessed due to a platform-side technical failure and could not be resolved within a reasonable timeframe.

In any of these situations, please contact us directly at [contact@cornerstonedirectory.com] so we can resolve it promptly.

---

### 7. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

We understand that chargebacks exist to protect consumers, and we respect that right. However, we ask that you **contact us before initiating a chargeback** with your bank or payment provider.

Most issues — including billing errors, unrecognized charges, and dissatisfied purchases — can be resolved quickly and directly. Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may result in:

- Immediate suspension of your Cornerstone Directory account
- Forfeiture of any referral credits associated with the disputed purchase
- A permanent ban from future membership, at our discretion

We're a small, faith-driven business and we take every concern seriously. If something isn't right, we genuinely want to fix it. Please give us the chance to do that first.
