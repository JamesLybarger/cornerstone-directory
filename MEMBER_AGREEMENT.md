## Member Agreement

### 1. Agreement Overview

This Member Agreement ("Agreement") is a binding contract between you ("Member") and Cornerstone Directory LLC ("Cornerstone Directory," "we," "us," or "our"), a limited liability company based in southern Utah.

This Agreement governs your participation as a **paid member** of the Cornerstone Directory platform — specifically, your right to list your business in the directory and to access the benefits associated with your membership tier. It is a separate document from Cornerstone Directory's general Terms of Service, which applies to all visitors and users of the platform. In the event of a conflict between this Agreement and the Terms of Service on matters specific to paid business listings, this Agreement controls.

**This Agreement takes effect** at the moment you complete your membership purchase and continues for the duration of your membership, whether that is a lifetime Founding Membership or an ongoing Annual Membership.

By purchasing a membership, you confirm that:

- You are at least 18 years of age;
- You have authority to list the business and agree to terms on its behalf;
- You have read and accept this Agreement in full.

### 2. Membership Types and Benefits

#### 2.1 Founding Member

Founding Members are among the first 500 members to join Cornerstone Directory under the Founding Member program. Founding Membership is available for a **one-time payment of $59.99** and grants **lifetime access** — no renewals, no annual fees, ever. Your membership remains active for as long as Cornerstone Directory operates, provided you remain in good standing under this Agreement.

#### 2.2 Annual Member

Annual Membership is available for **$59.99 per year**, billed on a recurring annual basis from the date of your initial purchase. Your membership renews automatically unless you cancel prior to your renewal date. You will receive a reminder before each renewal charge.

#### 2.3 Membership Benefits (Both Tiers)

All paid members — Founding and Annual — receive access to the following benefits, as they are made available on the platform:

| Benefit | Description |
|---|---|
| **Business Directory Listing** | A dedicated listing page for your business in the Cornerstone Directory, searchable by category, location, and keyword. |
| **Member Store Access** | Access to the Cornerstone Directory member store for discounted resources, tools, or partner offers made available to the community. |
| **Resource Library** | Access to the member resource library, which may include guides, templates, and educational content relevant to Christian business owners. |
| **Referral Program** | Participation in the referral program to earn credits toward renewal (see Section 6). |
| **Member Network** | Access to any member community or networking features offered through the platform. |

Cornerstone Directory may enhance, modify, or expand benefits over time. We will make reasonable efforts to notify members of significant changes to benefits. Removal of a core benefit without a comparable replacement will be communicated in advance.

### 3. Business Listing Requirements

#### 3.1 Required Information

To maintain an active listing in the directory, you must provide and keep current the following information:

- **Business name** (legal or trade name)
- **Business category** (selected from available directory categories)
- **Business description** (a clear, honest summary of the products or services you offer)
- **Primary contact information** (email address, phone number, or both)
- **Location or service area** (city/state at minimum; a full address is optional but encouraged)
- **Website URL** (if applicable)

You may also provide additional optional information such as social media profiles, business hours, and photos, which we encourage to make your listing as useful as possible to visitors.

#### 3.2 Accuracy Certification

By submitting and maintaining your listing, you certify that all information you provide is **true, accurate, and not misleading**. This includes your business name, description, contact information, category, and any claims made within your listing content. Cornerstone Directory relies on this certification and does not independently verify the accuracy of listing content.

#### 3.3 Obligation to Update

If any significant information in your listing changes — including your business name, primary contact information, location, or the nature of your services — you agree to update your listing **within 30 days** of that change. Allowing materially inaccurate information to remain in your listing is a violation of this Agreement.

#### 3.4 Categories and Organization

Listings are organized by business category as determined by Cornerstone Directory. You are responsible for selecting the most accurate category for your business. We reserve the right to recategorize a listing if the selected category is clearly inaccurate, and we will notify you if we do so. If you believe your business fits multiple categories, contact us at contact@cornerstonedirectory.com to discuss options.

### 4. Faith-Based Standards for Listed Businesses

#### 4.1 Our Foundation

Cornerstone Directory exists to connect customers with businesses that operate from a foundation of Christian faith and values. This is not simply a marketing label — it reflects our belief that how we conduct business matters, and that character, integrity, and care for others should shape every commercial relationship.

We are not a certifying body, and we do not audit your business practices. We extend trust to every member. But that trust comes with a sincere expectation.

#### 4.2 Member Affirmation

By joining Cornerstone Directory as a paid member, you affirm that:

- Your business is owned or operated by a person or persons who identify as Christian and who seek to conduct business in a manner consistent with Christian faith;
- Your business dealings are characterized by **honesty, transparency, and fair treatment** of customers, employees, vendors, and competitors;
- Your business does not knowingly engage in practices that are deceptive, exploitative, or harmful to others;
- You seek to bring integrity to your professional work as an expression of your faith.

This affirmation is not about theological perfection or denominational alignment — it is about the practical orientation of your business toward what is good, true, and honorable.

#### 4.3 What This Is Not

Membership in Cornerstone Directory is not:

- An endorsement, certification, or guarantee of your business by Cornerstone Directory;
- A claim that your business has been audited, reviewed, or verified by us;
- A warranty to customers about your products, services, or conduct.

We trust our members to represent themselves honestly. Cornerstone Directory's role is to provide a directory platform — the integrity of each listing rests with the member.

#### 4.4 Misrepresentation

If it becomes evident that a member has misrepresented their business as faith-aligned in order to gain access to the directory — without genuine intent to operate in accordance with Christian values — that misrepresentation is grounds for immediate removal from the directory. We do not police theology, but we take dishonesty seriously.

### 5. Prohibited Listing Content

The following content is not permitted in any business listing on Cornerstone Directory. Violations may result in listing removal or membership termination.

**5.1 False or Misleading Information**
You may not include false claims, fabricated credentials, misleading statistics, fake reviews, or any other content that creates a false impression of your business.

**5.2 Content Contrary to Christian Values**
Listings may not promote adult content, sexually explicit material, gambling services, illegal products or services, or any offering that is inconsistent with the faith-based character of this platform.

**5.3 Illegal Services or Products**
You may not list, describe, or advertise any business activity that is illegal under applicable federal, state, or local law.

**5.4 Competitor Advertising**
Your listing may not include advertisements, referrals, or promotional content for competing directory services or platforms.

**5.5 Spam and Keyword Stuffing**
Business descriptions must be genuine and readable. Artificially inflating a listing with excessive keywords, unrelated terms, or repetitive content designed to manipulate search visibility is prohibited.

**5.6 Third-Party Violations**
You may not use your listing to infringe on the trademarks, copyrights, or other intellectual property rights of any third party.

### 6. Referral Program Terms

#### 6.1 How Credits Are Earned

Active members may participate in the Cornerstone Directory referral program. When a person you refer purchases a new **Annual Membership**, you will receive a **$4.99 referral credit** applied to your account. Credits are issued after the referred member's purchase is confirmed and the payment clears.

#### 6.2 How Credits Are Applied

- **Annual Members:** Referral credits are applied toward your next annual renewal. Credits accumulate and can stack — if you have earned multiple credits, all available credits will be applied at your next renewal date.
- **Founding Members:** Because Founding Memberships do not renew, referral credits earned by Founding Members are tracked as **store credit** applicable to purchases in the Cornerstone Directory member store.

#### 6.3 No Cash Value

Referral credits have **no cash value** and may not be redeemed for cash, transferred to another person, or exchanged for any monetary equivalent.

#### 6.4 Forfeiture Upon Cancellation or Refund

All unused referral credits are **forfeited immediately** upon membership cancellation, refund, or termination — whether voluntary or for cause. Credits cannot be reinstated upon rejoining.

#### 6.5 Program Integrity

The referral program is designed to reward genuine introductions to the Cornerstone Directory community. The following are prohibited and may result in membership termination:

- Creating fake or fraudulent accounts to generate referral credits;
- Self-referral through secondary accounts;
- Offering compensation, kickbacks, or incentives to others specifically to trigger referral credits;
- Any other manipulation of the referral system not consistent with honest, good-faith participation.

#### 6.6 Program Changes

Cornerstone Directory reserves the right to modify or discontinue the referral program at any time with reasonable notice to members. Credits earned prior to any discontinuation will be honored for a reasonable period.

### 8. Listing Removal and Account Termination

#### 8.1 Grounds for Removal

Cornerstone Directory reserves the right to remove a business listing or terminate a membership for any of the following reasons:

- Violation of the faith-based standards set forth in Section 4;
- Submission of false or materially inaccurate listing information (Section 3);
- Inclusion of prohibited listing content (Section 5);
- Violation of the referral program integrity provisions (Section 6.5);
- Abusive, harassing, or grossly inappropriate conduct toward staff or other members (Section 7);
- Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute in bad faith after receiving the agreed-upon membership access;
- Any conduct that, in our reasonable judgment, is harmful to the Cornerstone Directory community or its members.

#### 8.2 Notice Process

In most cases, before removing a listing or terminating a membership, we will:

1. Notify the member by email identifying the concern;
2. Allow a reasonable opportunity (typically 7 days) to respond or cure the issue.

However, in cases involving severe violations — including fraud, illegal activity, egregious content, or conduct that poses an immediate risk to the community — we reserve the right to remove a listing or terminate a membership immediately and without prior notice.

#### 8.3 No Refund Upon Termination for Cause

If your membership is terminated because you violated this Agreement, **no refund will be issued** for any portion of your membership fee. Termination for cause does not entitle you to a prorated refund.

If you voluntarily cancel your Annual Membership, our standard refund policy (as described in the Terms of Service) applies.
